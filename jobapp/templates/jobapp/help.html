Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button on the top right corner and fill in your details.

2. How do I apply for a job?

Browse jobs under the 'Jobs' section and click 'Apply Now' for the job you want.

3. How can recruiters post a job?

Recruiters need to log in and go to the 'Post a Job' page to submit job details.
