JobPortal

JobPortal is the #1 job site and a global leader in job matching and hiring, operating in over 60 countries. More people find jobs on Indeed than anywhere else because we put job seekers first—offering powerful tools to search jobs, post resumes, research companies, and more.

Powered by AI and extensive proprietary jobs and hiring data, Indeed is transforming how people and employers connect. Every day, we help tens of millions of job seekers discover new opportunities and empower millions of employers to find the right talent through innovative AI-driven solutions that simplify and accelerate the hiring process. Indeed is a subsidiary of Recruit Holdings, a global leader in HR technology and business solutions that is simplifying hiring and transforming the world of work.
